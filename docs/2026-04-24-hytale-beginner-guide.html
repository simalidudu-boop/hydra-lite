What is Hytale?


Hytale is a voxel-based sandbox game, similar to Minecraft, but with its own unique twist. It allows players to build, explore, and survive in a procedurally generated world, complete with its own set of rules, mechanics, and features. The game is designed to be highly customizable, with a strong focus on community engagement and user-generated content.
