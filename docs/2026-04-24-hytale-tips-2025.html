What is Hytale?


Hytale is a sandbox game that allows players to build, explore, and survive in a procedurally generated world. The game features a unique block-based system, similar to Minecraft, but with a greater emphasis on creativity and customization. Players can build anything from simple structures to complex machines, using a wide range of blocks and materials.
