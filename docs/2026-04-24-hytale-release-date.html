What is Hytale?

Hytale is a massively multiplayer online (MMO) game developed by Hypixel Studios, a game development company founded by Alexander "ap0c" Levin and the Hypixel team. Hytale is a sandbox-style game that allows players to explore, build, and interact with a vast, dynamic world.
